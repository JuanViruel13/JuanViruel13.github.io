Three facts about Mars


  1. Mars is often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance, which is caused by iron oxide, or rust, on its surface.

  2. Mars has two small moons named Phobos and Deimos. These moons are irregularly shaped and are thought to be captured asteroids.

  3. Mars is the fourth planet in our solar system, located between Earth and Jupiter. It is approximately 142 million miles (228 million kilometers) away from the Sun.

For more information about Mars, please visit Wikipedia.
